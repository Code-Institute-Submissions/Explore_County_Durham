Explore County Durham

County Durham

Durham is a beautiful county in North East England, with breathtaking scenery and a multitude of attractions, you will never get bored exploring this wonderful area. From the tranquil coastline to the rugged wilderness of the Durham Dales, majestic castles and beautiful villages, there is so much on offer to keep you coming back for more.

Visitor Attractions

Explore Durham city, with it's world famous cathedral and castle, take a step back in time and visit Beamish, the living museum of the North. Go and see the wonderful waterfall at High Force, there's so much to see and do all over our beautiful county.
